About Me

I’m a Civil Engineer passionate about merging Artificial Intelligence with Civil Engineering to build sustainable, intelligent infrastructure. My research interests include AI-based Structural Health Monitoring, Pavement Temperature Analysis, and Digital Twin Development for resilient cities.
